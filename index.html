Mint-Chai

Mint tea, a popular herbal infusion, is cherished for its refreshing taste, aromatic qualities, and numerous health benefits. This caffeine-free beverage is made by steeping fresh or dried mint leaves in hot water, releasing a soothing aroma and a cool, invigorating flavor. Mint tea has been enjoyed for centuries across various cultures, from the traditional Moroccan mint tea ceremonies to its use in traditional medicine. Flavor and Preparation Mint tea boasts a naturally sweet and crisp flavor, making it a delightful drink both hot and cold. To prepare mint tea, simply add a handful of fresh mint leaves or a teaspoon of dried mint leaves to a cup of boiling water.
